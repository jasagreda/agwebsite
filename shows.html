TV Shows & Movies

"Victorious" is a TV show about Tori Vega, a high school student attending a performing arts school. The show combines humor, music, and teenage drama, highlighting the characters' experiences as they pursue their dreams.

"Sam & Cat" is a spin-off of two Nickelodeon shows, "iCarly" and "Victorious," featuring characters Sam Puckett (from "iCarly") and Cat Valentine (from "Victorious") as roommates and babysitters. The show follows their comedic adventures as they start a babysitting business together.

"Scream Queens" follows a group of sorority sisters at Wallace University who are targeted by a masked killer. Filled with dark humor, satire, and over-the-top drama, the series explores the mystery behind the murders and the secrets of the characters.

"Hairspray Live!" is based on the Broadway musical "Hairspray," which in turn was based on the 1988 film of the same name. The production received praise for its faithful adaptation of the beloved story about racial integration and acceptance in 1960s Baltimore.

"Excuse Me, I Love You" is a concert documentary film featuring Ariana Grande, showcasing her performances from the "Sweetener World Tour." It offers a blend of musical performances, intimate moments, and a closer look at Grande's artistry and connection with her fans.
