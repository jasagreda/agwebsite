The Listening Sessions Tour was the debut concert tour by American singer Ariana Grande. It supported her debut studio album, "Yours Truly," and featured songs from the album as well as some covers. The tour began on August 11, 2013, in Silver Spring, Maryland, and concluded on September 22, 2013, in Burbank, California. Grande performed in various cities across North America, including New York City, Los Angeles, and Toronto. The tour received positive reviews from critics, who praised Grande's vocal ability and stage presence.

The Dangerous Woman Tour was the third concert tour by Ariana Grande, in support of her third studio album, "Dangerous Woman." The tour began on February 3, 2017, in Phoenix, Arizona, and concluded on September 21, 2017, in Hong Kong. Grande performed in North America, Europe, Latin America, Asia, and Oceania. The tour received praise for Grande's vocal performance, stage presence, and choreography. It was also a commercial success, selling out in many locations and grossing over $71 million.

The Sweetener World Tour was the fourth concert tour by Ariana Grande, in support of her fourth and fifth studio albums, "Sweetener" and "Thank U, Next." The tour began on March 18, 2019, in Albany, New York, and concluded on December 22, 2019, in Inglewood, California. Grande performed in North America, Europe, and Asia. The tour received critical acclaim for Grande's vocal performance, stage production, and emotional depth. It was also a commercial success, grossing over $146 million and selling out in many locations.
